title EMAIL SENDER SEQUENCE

Client -> Client: Collect user email
Client -> Client: Prepare request to API containing user's email
Client -> Email Sender: Send contact request
Email Sender -> Email Sender: Receive user's email
Email Sender -> Email Worker: Add email to QUEUE
Email Worker -> Email Worker: Consume message from queue
Email Worker -> Email Worker: Retrieve my relevant info
Email Worker -> Email Worker: Send email with relevant info


Email Sender -> Email Sender: Store user email on db
Email Sender -> Client: send response
Client -> Client: Display message saying that the email should arrive soon
